## Description
One Turn is a minimalist puzzle game built around a single decisive gesture.

See the board. Read the line. Swipe once.

Your piece glides until the board stops it, bends it, or transforms the route. To solve a level, you must activate every required mark in that one uninterrupted motion.

What makes it addictive:

- One move means every board is instantly readable.
- Restarts are immediate, so failure feels crisp instead of punishing.
- Daily puzzles give you a hard, shareable ritual.
- Endless mode rewards momentum, chain play, and “one more run.”
- Studio packs introduce elegant mechanics without clutter.

Included in version 1:

- Daily Puzzle with streak tracking
- Endless Mode with escalating challenge
- Studio packs with curated progression
- Tap-to-place planning boards for harder puzzles
- Subtle haptics, restrained sound, and accessibility options

One Turn is designed to feel like a gallery object that turns into a habit.
