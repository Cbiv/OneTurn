# One Turn Beta Checklist

## Core Stability
- Launch on iPhone 12, 13, 14, 15, and 16 class devices.
- Confirm no letterboxing or black bars on Home, Daily, Endless, Studio, Settings, Stats, and Themes.
- Cold launch, background, foreground, and relaunch three times per device.

## Gameplay
- Swipe detection resolves cleanly to a cardinal direction.
- Restart is immediate after fail on every mode.
- Planning puzzles allow legal mirror placement anywhere permitted and animate smoothly after swipe.
- Daily first clear increments streak once and only once.
- Endless run exits cleanly to Home without losing the finalized score.

## Product Surfaces
- App icon appears correctly on Home Screen and in App Library.
- Sound toggle fully mutes gameplay tones.
- Analytics toggle disables new event recording.
- Stats screen shows launch count, crash-free beta rate, and core solve metrics.

## Beta Exit Criteria
- 99%+ crash-free launches across internal beta sessions.
- No clipped board layouts on supported iPhones.
- No blocker bugs in Daily, Endless, or Studio.
- At least 20 completed beta runs with no progression corruption.
